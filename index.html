Our Services

What we provide as a company

1.Condition-Based Maintenance: At CurrentSolutions, we believe in the power of proactive maintenance to optimize the performance and reliability of your electrical systems. Our thermography inspections utilize cutting-edge infrared imaging technology to assess equipment condition and predict potential failures. By detecting issues early, we minimize downtime, reduce costs, and enhance safety.

2.Preventive Maintenance Inspections: Our skilled thermographers conduct comprehensive assessments of your electrical equipment and systems to identify potential issues before they escalate. Through detailed inspections and infrared imaging, we provide actionable recommendations to avoid breakdowns, optimize performance, and extend equipment lifespan.

3.Energy Efficiency Audits: Our energy efficiency audits help businesses optimize energy usage and reduce costs. We assess electrical systems, identify wastage, and provide recommendations for upgrades, power management strategies, and energy-saving technologies.

4.Troubleshooting and Diagnostics: When electrical problems occur, our experienced thermographers offer efficient troubleshooting and diagnostics services. We pinpoint root causes, such as overheating components or circuit failures, and provide actionable solutions to restore system functionality.
